Year 4 Project

This project will be focusing on the development of a Medical Education E-Library for hospital staff use. The goal of this project is to provide easy access to training materials and medical device specific safety documentation. This project will be addressing challenges such as training accessibility during night shifts, communications for the distribution of notifications and maintaining upkeep of resources on medical devices to keep up to date. The end result of this project will also help reduce paper waste, transitioning from hardcopy manuals to eco-friendly digital resources.
